About Me

Welcome to my website! I'm Jeffrey Hanschmann, a Fellow of the Casualty Actuarial Society (FCAS) and a Member of the American Academy of Actuaries (MAAA). For the past ten years, I have been growing my career in business and analytics as it pertains to the insurance industry and actuarial insights. I currently work for Guy Carpenter as a reinsurance actuary in Seattle, WA, USA. For more information on my insurance and actuarial background, visit my LinkedIn page.

I would describe myself as process-oriented and goal-focused, with experience spanning several business units within insurance companies and spanning several countries, from North America to EMEA to Asia Pacific. My main interest lies at the intersection of insurance solutions, streamline processing, and product development. My goal is quite simple: Make insurance solutions and insights simple and readily available for those who want to protect what they truly love.

My hobbies outside of my profession include personal real estate investment, personal finance organization and planning, home-cooking and baking, Seattle Wiffleball, tennis, and resource replacement boardgames.
